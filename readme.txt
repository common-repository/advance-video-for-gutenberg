= Gutenberg Advance Video =

This plugin adds a new video block to the Gutenberg editor with the following features:

** Drag and drop: Upload and add videos to your Page/Post by just dragging them to the editor.
** Autoplay:Make your videos autoplay as soon as they are loaded
** Loop: Repeat the video automatically when it is over.
** Controls: Show/hide the video controls
